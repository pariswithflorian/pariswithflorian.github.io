Paris with Florian

Private Cultural Tours with a Scholar and Author

About Dr. Florian

I am a scholar and author specializing in anthropology and philosophy, with contributions to over ten published books and more than a thousand pages of academic writing. Fluent in four languages, I invite you to explore Paris beyond its surface — through its ideas, contradictions, and cultural layers.

This work is also part of my broader research practice. As the founder of an interdisciplinary research lab in Paris, I see each encounter as an opportunity to share my passion for Paris — its history, its lived practice, and its evolving identity — not as a guide, but as a researcher and flâneur shaped by the city itself.

The Experience

Each tour is an intimate intellectual journey through the city's art, architecture, and historical topography — tailored to your interests and guided by conversation. Rather than generic commentary, I offer depth, curiosity, and connection. These are not scripted tours, but curated encounters with Paris.
